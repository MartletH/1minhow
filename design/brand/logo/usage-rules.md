# 1minHow Logo Usage Rules v0.1

## Current authority

Use the currently published `1minHow` mark without redrawing it until a later reviewed brand revision replaces this version.

## Required preservation

- Keep the original proportions.
- Preserve the exact capitalization `1minHow`.
- Use approved source exports only; do not recreate the mark from typed text.
- Maintain sufficient contrast against the background.
- Keep surrounding decorative characters and theme elements visually secondary.

## Prohibited changes

- no stretching, squashing, skewing or rotation;
- no independent movement or recoloring of letters;
- no outline, shadow, glow or gradient unless a later version explicitly approves it;
- no replacement of the lettering with a look-alike font;
- no embedding inside a character illustration;
- no use of public-domain painting motifs as part of the core mark without a reviewed logo revision.

## Theme integration

The three visual families may decorate nearby space but do not alter the core logo:

- Doodle / 星空生态合唱: small distributed characters or stars may sit outside the clear-space boundary.
- Linework / 宇宙: restrained orbital or cosmic linework may frame the surrounding layout, not cross the mark.
- Ink / 山海: ink, mountain, sea and cloud accents may sit behind or beside the mark only when contrast remains exact.

## Bilingual use

Chinese explanatory text may appear as a separate label. It must not be fused into the core mark until a bilingual lockup is intentionally designed, versioned and reviewed.

## Change rule

Any change to lettering, proportions, spacing, color construction or lockup creates a new immutable logo asset version and changelog entry. Historical files are never overwritten.
